Honeypot Detection

In the blockchain community, malicious attackers started luring other attackers into contracts that seem to have exploitable flaws, but that actually contain a complex hidden trap that in the end benefits the contract creator and these contracts are known as honeypots. We try to establish effective honeypot detection mechanisms to identify and mitigate potential risks.
